## Integrate Wallet (ReiWallet)

For developers looking to integrate browser extension wallets into their dApps, we currently recommend using Rei Wallet.

Rei Wallet is a chrome extension for CKB and RGB++ chains, please visit [Tutorial](https://docs.reiwallet.io/docs/tutorial/simple-dapp) to integrate.

Stay tuned for future integration guides for additional wallets as our ecosystem continues to grow.
